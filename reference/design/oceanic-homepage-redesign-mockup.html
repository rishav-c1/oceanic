What we do

One consultant, accountable for every system in the building.

Strategy, execution and commissioning held together by impeccable, time-bound monitoring, so newcomers and veterans alike build with confidence.

01

Planning & Layouts

Conceptual planning, area calculation and feasible layouts conforming to clinical-establishment rules and NABH requirements.

02

Electrical & Power

Total load calculation (raw, generator and UPS) with wiring layouts, contractor liaison and installation oversight.

03

HVAC & Fire

Cost-benefit-led HVAC selection by temperature and air-change need, plus fire-fighting installation and commissioning.

04

Licensing

Identifying and documenting every licence (Fire, Pollution Control, PNDT, AERB, Clinical and Trade) and guiding procurement.

05

Equipment & IT

Department-wise equipment evaluation, vendor comparatives, HMS and networking selection, layout and commissioning.

06

HR, Finance & Ops

Manpower planning, salary structures, training, inventory and finance systems to run the setup from day one.
